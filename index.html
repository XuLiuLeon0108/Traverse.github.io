Why Traverse?

Most of us have thousands of photos, but barely remember the stories behind them. Traverse is designed as a memory-first app: instead of raw images, it helps you build stories tied to time, place, and narrative.

Key Features

Story-based Memories

Create structured stories composed of segments, each with text, timestamps, and optional images — so every trip, meetup, or moment becomes a readable narrative.

Time & Location Context

Capture where and when your memories happen using one-shot location access, inspired by how the hippocampus links place and time.

Clean & Focused Interface

Built with Material 3 & Jetpack Compose for a calm, distraction-free journaling experience.

Local-first Storage

Powered by Room database. Your memories are stored on-device for fast access and better control.

UI Preview

Traverse includes a landing screen, story list, story editor, and location-aware cards to help you browse and build memories intuitively.
